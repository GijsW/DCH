About this community

This group was founded on 22-02-2014 by Alfred Pellegrino. After playing Battlefield 3 solo for a few months in 2010 I joined The Hardcore Clan. Several months later i noticed that this clan was dead, although me and two other players played a lot. We met a good friend at that clan and still am in contact with him, Scare-NL. I left the clan and ended up with the Brothers of Benelux. This was a fun clan but when BE-Scorpion aka SeriallKillers aka Gen-Scorpion ( this dude changes his nickname every other day...lol ) left, the group fell apart. I switched to the Benelux Fighters Clan which BE-Scorpion aka SeriallKillers aka Gen-Scorpion had founded. After a while, the name of this clan changed to Dogs Of War. Then after some thinking we decided that it was time to go official and we founded an official community. In January 2014, BE-Scorpion aka Dimitry VDB aka SeriallKillers aka Gen-Scorpion the community ( whilst still being a board-member) after a disagreement with me. Because BE-Scorpion aka SeriallKillers aka Gen-Scorpion left and took most of our group, Dogs Of War was left bleeding to death. We, as in Dion and I, then decided to leave the community as board-members as well. This decision was taken after there was no longer any contact with the last board-member. I joined the NextRevolution for a little while but didn't quite feel at ease there so I left. After being rejected by the Addict-Clan I decided that it was time to try to do it on my own.
Don't know where this is gonna end up but sometimes you just have to start something. We have a Teamspeak-server and two game-servers. Our teamspeak link can be found in the left menu. You can find our platoon of Battlefield 3 and Battlefield 4 by clicking these links. What is our goal? Our goal is to game together in an kind and fun way. With our TeamSpeak we can communicate and discuss tactics, but the TeamSpeak is also for interacting with our other members.
